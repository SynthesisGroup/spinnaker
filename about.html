About Landco

Providing premium and sustainable developments that will have a worth in the world and in people’s lives

Known for pioneering premium developments for more than 30 years, Landco Pacific Corporation has created world-class leisure communities, breathtaking resort-inspired condominiums, and luxury home communities in the Philippines. The premier real estate developer continues to provide premium and sustainable developments that will have worth in the world and people’s lives.

As a subsidiary of the Metro Pacific Investments Corporation (MPIC) with business tycoon Manuel V. Pangilinan at its helm and its long-standing reputation for business stability and undisputed growth, Landco will continually create distinctive landscapes of leisure and lifestyle worth bequeathing to the next generations of families while fully enjoying them now. Landco Pacific properties are the consummate investment: precious, priceless, prestigious, and continually yielding.

Landco Pacific has diverse premium developments throughout the country. Its flagship development, Peninsula de Punta Fuego in Nasugbu, Batangas is the country’s first private and exclusive seaside residential resort. Landco’s latest BeachTowns are master-planned and sustainable seaside residential and resort communities in prime tourist and leisure spots of Batangas featuring CaSoBe (Calatagan South Beach) and Club Laiya.

About The Spinnaker

A stunning, medium-size beachside condominium facing the glorious sea on one side and a lush mountain range on the other. Named after a special boat sail, the Spinnaker looks and feels like a sail in full breeze, its glitz and momentum pushing upwards.
